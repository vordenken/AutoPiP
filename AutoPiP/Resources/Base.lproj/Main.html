Features

Configure these anytime in the AutoPiP Safari toolbar popup.

Automatic PiP Switch tabs or windows – your video continues in PiP.
Scroll PiP Scroll past a YouTube video to trigger PiP automatically.
Keyboard Shortcut Press ⌥P to toggle PiP at any time (customizable).
Site Control Blacklist mode (default) works on all sites. Switch to Whitelist to allow only specific ones.

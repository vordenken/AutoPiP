- Blacklist / Whitelist mode to control which sites AutoPiP is active on
- Updated Sparkle to 2.9.0

- Fixed issue where clicking into YouTube live chat would incorrectly activate PiP mode
- Improved focus detection to prevent PiP activation on internal page interactions
